# Code Of Conduct

This project should be a practical and respectful place to discuss Android
automation, scripting, OCR, and related development work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient with other users and contributors.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and legitimate local automation use.
- Share enough technical detail to help others reproduce or understand a
  problem.
- Redact private screenshots, account data, tokens, passwords, signing keys, and
  personal information before posting.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Posting private data, secrets, exploit payloads, or sensitive screenshots.
- Requests to steal data, bypass access controls, abuse another app or service,
  or evade platform rules.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues; block users; or
limit participation when behavior harms the project or its users.

If you see a safety or security problem, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
instead of posting sensitive details publicly.
